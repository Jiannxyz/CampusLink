About CampusLink

Built for students, schools, and communities

CampusLink connects schools and students in one trusted space. It supports campus interaction, highlights events and organizations, and makes inter-school networking simple — so digital academic communities can grow with clarity and purpose.

  • Share updates and discover what is happening on nearby campuses.
  • Promote events, clubs, and initiatives with a social feed experience.
  • Represent your school while exploring broader student networks.
  • Stay organized with tools designed for academic life online.
