About CampusLink

CampusLink is an inter-school social networking and event platform. It connects students, schools, and organizations so everyone can see what’s happening beyond a single classroom or bulletin board.

Schools and groups can share events, announcements, and campus activities in one trusted space. Students follow feeds, RSVP to events, and explore communities — encouraging collaboration across campuses and stronger student engagement.

Whether you’re promoting a club fair, a lecture series, or a weekend meetup, CampusLink helps your message reach the right people at the right time.

  • Cross-campus visibility
    Public and school-scoped content so communities can share safely.
  • Events at the center
    Discover what’s on and save what you care about.
  • Built for real communities
    Profiles, organizations, and feeds designed around campus life.
